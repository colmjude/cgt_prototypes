Terms and conditions for internal applications

If the role you’re applying for is a level transfer you’ll be able to keep your existing terms and conditions.

If this is an internal promotion, you’ll be:

  • entitled to sick pay of one month’s full pay and one month’s half pay in your first year (rising to a maximum of 5 months full pay and 5 months half pay after 5 years)
  • considered eligible for a compulsory posting to another department in the UK (also known as ‘mobile’)
  • transferred to the new Cabinet Office terms and conditions
  • able to claim excess business costs
  • allowed to retain existing terms and conditions if it’s a level transfer
